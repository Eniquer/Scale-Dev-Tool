Welcome

This AI‑assisted tool guides you through end‑to‑end scale development—from defining your construct to modeling, pretesting, and refinement.

How to proceed:
  1. Step 1 · Conceptualization — Define the construct, domain, and subdimensions.
  2. Step 2 · Item Generation — Create candidate items manually or with AI assist.
  3. Step 3 · Content Validity — Have raters assess item–construct fit.
  4. Step 4 · Model Specification — Choose reflective/formative facets and auto‑generate lavaan spec.
  5. Step 5 · Pretest — Compile a questionnaire and simulate or collect responses.
  6. Step 6 · Purification — Reverse‑key, run EFA/CFA, and refine the scale.
Use the left sidebar to navigate steps. You can export progress anytime from the top bar.
