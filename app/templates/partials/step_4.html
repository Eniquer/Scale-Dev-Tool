Listing subdimensions defined in Step 1. For each facet, specify whether items reflect (reflective) or form (formative) the facet.

Reflective: items are effects; interchangeable; internal consistency (e.g., Cronbach's alpha) is meaningful.
Formative: items are causes; non-interchangeable; internal consistency is not required nor expected.
